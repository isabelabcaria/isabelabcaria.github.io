Grand Adventure Catalunya
IMAGEM

Tourism is a strategic sector and one of the economic driving forces of the Catalan economy. How can tourists easily discover unique places tourism providers attract more tourists and avoid seasonality. Grand Adventure Catalunya is a scalable solution that inspires change in a post pandemic world.

ROLE

Research, concept development and business strategy

OUTPUT

A gamified loyalty program that connects the client to tourists and tourism providers through a mobile and a web app.

COLLABORATORS

Catalunya is a diverse territory, with extensive mountains, inland depressions, and a coastline that stretches for 214 km.
