Grand Adventure Catalunya

Tourism is a strategic sector and one of the economic driving forces of the Catalan economy. How can tourists easily discover unique places tourism providers attract more tourists and avoid seasonality. Grand Adventure Catalunya is a scalable solution that inspires change in a post pandemic world.

Role

Research, concept development and business strategy

Output

A gamified loyalty program that connects the client to tourists and tourism providers through a mobile and a web app.

Collaborators

Corina Vladut, Kristelle Achkar, Carla Camerlingo, Vitor Gobatto and Jeroen Ernst​

Context

Catalunya is a diverse territory, with extensive mountains, inland depressions, and a coastline that stretches for 214 km. Its provinces are very rich in natural scenery!

The most important and powerful initiative for promoting tourism in the land is the Catalan Tourist Board (CTB), a team that conducts promotional activities and commercial actions from both the headquarters in Catalunya and tourist promotion offices distributed around the world.

Challenge

The project we worked on was the most important one in the last 5 years. It intends to change radiacally the offer and structure of the tourism sector in the region as it aims to spread tourism beyond the most popular spots, better distributing expenditure across all provinces and reducing seasonality.

The challenge was to contribute with a complete solution in which the main objective was to bring tourists to regions that today do not receive them, helping small business owners to enter the tourist sector and to survive on what they offer in their region.

Process

In normal situations the team would conduct field research, however, considering the pandemic and its repercussions we had to replan all the research process with tourists and tourism providers. Suddenly, we (as the rest of the world) had to adapt, as humans, as a team and as designers to embrace this new challenge of working and interacting remotely!

Findings

We found out that travelers want to know the local, unique, real experiences away from tourists, thus many travel in non touristic periods.

In light of this, the team ideated on how to create a way for tourists to discover these unique experiences while helping tourism providers manage their offers and balance demand in a way that it is aligned with the CTB strategy. These needs made us think in terms of ecosystems in order to truly generate value.

Solution

Mobile app for tourists

A gamified experience for tourists visiting Catalunya. Through the app travelers can book attractions, discover secret spots and unique places as well as beautiful landscapes while:

- Acumulating roses (traditional Catalan symbol that was used to represent the points with the aim of bringing the tourists closer to the local culture); - Leveling up (becoming characters that are experts in gastronomy, nature and others); - Getting rewards.

PROTOTYPE MOBILE APP

Concept

​​The concept was based on the idea of variable reward from product psychology. It involves moving from a conventional feedback loop to a new habit-forming mechanism to hook users: the Hook Model.

Variable rewards apparently keep the brain busy, clearing away its defenses and opening space for generating new habits. The idea is based on the reward of the self, of the tribe and of the hunt.

Solution

Web app for tourists

The idea was based on the concept of yield management which is typically used by airlines. Most of them sell seats for a variety of different fares, the earlier the customer makes the reservation, lower fares might be available, but if they decide to make a last minute booking, they will probably pay the full fare. This way, fixed capacity of seats is allocated in the most profitable manner possible.

The concept developed derives from this idea by organizing tourist flow across the regions and connecting tourism providers with this information.

Impact

• We made the concept operational and designed the strategy through the Blueprint and the Business Model Canvas. This grounded the ideas to the client. • We delivered a gamified experience to the client that went beyond their expectations besides being a scalable solution. • The Yield Management concept inspired in the CTB with future directions for the tourism sector. • We generated meaningful discussions around the changes in tourism specially in a post pandemic world. • The project is still being developed and might be implemented within the next one or two years.
