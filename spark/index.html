Spark: the magic in remote work

We’re living crazy times and work life isn’t always what it used to be. Working from home has its perks, but it sure comes with some challanges as well. Small talks through the corridors, laughs by the coffee machine, unwinding at lunch with colleagues after a long and busy morning of work...Who would've thought we would miss that so much? Spark believes that relationships in virtual spaces should be as inspiring and human as in real life.

Role

Research
Concept development
UI

Output

A digital experience that matches coworkers according to their daily emotions, encourages taking breaks and facilitates taking initiative to connect remotely.
